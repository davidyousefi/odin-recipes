Pizza dish

Showcase of the simple dish made with bread, cheese, tomato sauce and a meat of your choice. It is simple and cheap to make making it valuable stable dish to have in your repertoire as you can add multiple things later on as you perfect the dish

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Meat of your choice
  • Spices
  • Vegetables (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees
  2. Put bread on a baking tray
  3. Put tomato sauce on the bread
  4. Put cheese on the bread
  5. Put meat on the bread
  6. Put spices on the bread
  7. Put the pizza in the oven
  8. Let it bake for 20 minutes
  9. Take the pizza out of the oven
  10. Put vegetables on the bread (optional)
