Spaghetti and meatsauce dish

Showcase of the simple dish made with spaghetti and a meatsauce. It is simple and cheap to make making it valuable stable dish to have in your repertoire as you can add multiple things later on as you perfect the dish

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Spices
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Put spaghetti in the pot
  3. Chop onions and garlic
  4. Put ground beef in a pan and cook it
  5. Put onions and garlic in the pan
  6. Put tomato sauce in the pan
  7. Put spices in the pan
  8. Let it simmer until ready
  9. Put the spaghetti and meatsauce on a plate
  10. Put cheese on top (optional)
